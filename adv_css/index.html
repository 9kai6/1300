Welcome to the world of "Fengshen Trilogy"

"The Three Kingdoms of Gods" is a costume fantasy series directed by Wuershan and starring Fei Xiang, Li Xuejian, Huang Bo, Yu Shi, Chen Muchi, Na Ran, Xia Yu, and Yuan Quan. The first part was released in mainland China on July 20, 2023, and the second part was released in mainland China on January 29, 2025.

About the Movie

The series is adapted from the Ming Dynasty novel "The Investiture of the Gods" and the Song and Yuan Dynasty vernacular novel "The Story of King Wu's Conquest of King Zhou". It consists of three films: "The Investiture of the Gods Part I: Chaoge Storm", "The Investiture of the Gods Part II: War in Xiqi" and "The Investiture of the Gods Part III". The first part tells the story of the fox demon appearing in the world, King Zhou's tyranny, and the chaos in the world; the second part tells the story of the Yin Shang army approaching Xiqi, and Jiang Ziya and other Kunlun immortals helping Ji Fa to fight back; the third part tells the story of Ji Fa uniting with other princes to attack Yin Shou, Yuanshi Tianzun and the twelve golden immortals coming down the mountain, and the war between the three realms of humans, immortals and demons further escalates.

Meet the Characters

  • In the film, Daji is a concubine of the Shang Dynasty and is considered one of the female characters who brought disaster to the country and the people. In the film, Daji is portrayed as a very charming and complex female character. Her relationship with King Zhou of Shang and her evil aura add a mysterious touch to the film. Daji's character is not simply a villain. Her complexity makes the audience feel both hostile and sympathetic towards her.
  • King Zhou was the last monarch of the Shang Dynasty, known for his tyranny and debauchery. In the movie, King Zhou not only showed a tyrannical ruling style, but also showed an extreme desire and fear for power. He was a very contradictory character, with strong political and military talents on the one hand, and indulged in pleasure on the other. Chen Kun's interpretation added a lot of layers to King Zhou, making this character look both evil and tragic.
  • Yin Jiao is a prince of the Shang Dynasty, a skilled warrior, but a tragic character. In the story, Yin Jiao eventually embarks on a path of rebellion under the guidance of his father, King Zhou, and becomes one of the main enemies of the Zhou uprising. Despite his outstanding talents and courage, he suffers a tragic fate in the Conferred Gods War and eventually dies in battle.
  • Ji Fa is one of the core characters in The Investiture of the Gods. He is the son of the legendary King Wen of Zhou and later the founder of the Zhou Dynasty - King Wu of Zhou. He is a hero in the story, with great wisdom and leadership skills, and is also the representative of justice in the play.

Legacy

The Investiture of the Gods trilogy is a grand epic film series directed by Wuershan, adapted from the ancient Chinese classic novel Investiture of the Gods. The production of this trilogy has attracted widespread attention in the Chinese film industry, combining traditional mythological stories with modern film technology to create a visually stunning and plot-rich work.
