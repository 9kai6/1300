A Portrait of Jianghu: Bad Guys

Li Xingyun, the hero of the fantasy martial arts animation series "Painting Rivers and Lakes: The Bad Guys", is based on the historical Tang Dynasty's last emperor Li Ye and the queen's son Li Xiang. He appeared as a guest character in the animations "Painting Rivers and Lakes: The Student Who Changes the World" and "Painting Rivers and Lakes: Xia Lan".

characters

In the animation "Painting Jianghu: The Bad Guys", the character settings of the four zombie ancestors are based on the zombie ancestors in ancient Chinese mythology: Ying Gou, Hou Qing, Hanba and Jiang Chen. These mythological elements add a deep cultural background to the characters.
