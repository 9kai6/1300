Welcome to the world of One Piece

"One Piece" is an adventure manga created by Japanese manga artist Eiichiro Oda, and was first serialized in "Weekly Shonen Jump" on July 22, 1997. The manga tells the story of the protagonist Monkey D. Luffy leading his companions on an adventure to find the legendary treasure "ONE PIECE" in order to become the Pirate King.

About this anime

The story is set in a vast ocean world where pirates run rampant. Various strange abilities, dangerous creatures, mysterious treasures and sea adventures form the core of the story. Luffy and his companions (known as the "Straw Hat Pirates") travel through various dangerous islands and seas, solve mysteries one by one, challenge the world's strongest enemies, and gradually approach the goal of becoming the Pirate King.

Meet the Characters

  • Zoro is one of the important characters in "One Piece" and a swordsman of the Straw Hat Pirates. Initially, he was a lone swordsman with a dream of swordsmanship, and later became Luffy's partner. His goal is to become the "strongest swordsman in the world" and to defeat the current strongest swordsman, Hawkeye Mihawk.
  • Yamato is a member of the "Hundred Beasts Pirates" and the son of Kaido (Kaido once had a female incarnation). Yamato has been disgusted with her father Kaido since she was a child, especially with his oppressive rule and tyranny. She admires "Fire Fist" Ace (Luffy's brother) and hopes to be a free person like him. Yamato finally joined the Straw Hat Pirates and formed a deep friendship with Luffy.
  • Boa Hancock is one of the most attractive female characters in "One Piece". She is the queen of Amazon Lily Island and the captain of the "Nine Snakes Pirates". Hancock is not only the leader of the pirate group, but also one of the "Seven Warlords of the Sea" of the World Government. Her appearance and abilities make her a super popular character in the story.

Achievement

"One Piece" has not only achieved great success in the comics field, but also spawned animations, movies, games and other forms. The global comic sales have exceeded 500 million copies, making it one of the best-selling comics in the world. At the same time, "One Piece" has also won many awards and is one of the representative works of Japanese anime.
